Honey Turkey

Delicious Honey Glazed Turkey

Looking to jazz up your turkey this Thanksgiving? Well, then this slightly spicy, slightly sweet, fully-flavor-packed, Easy Spicy Honey Glazed Turkey is for you!

ingredients

  • 1.5-1.9kg small turkey crown, defrosted
  • 50g butter, softened
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 clementines, 2 zested and juiced, 2 halved
  • 60ml clear honey
